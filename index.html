About Us

We help small or medium enterprises, start-ups and NGOs to look for employees at an affordable salary by connecting them with unskilled/skilled high school and university students through an online platform.

Our Services

  • 1. We help fresh start-up, a small or medium business, or a non-profit organization on a tight budget searching for well trained interns to pay at a reasonable rate.

  • 2. We, as an employment agency will also be the one conducting interviews and recruitment process for start-up, small or medium business, or non-profit organization who are looking to recruit interns. All you have to do is email as your requirements and details regarding the interns you are looking to recruit.

  • 3. For Companies We assist new start-ups, small and medium businesses, and non-profit organizations that are on a tight budget and need well-trained interns who are willing to work for a reasonable wage. All you have to do is send an email with your requirements and information about the interns you're looking to hire.

    For Students CamSEED is also a student-friendly platform for unskilled or skilled high school and university students to look for paid-internships.
